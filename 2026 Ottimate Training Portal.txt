        <li><strong>Unlimited User Model:</strong> There are no user license fees, allowing clients to add as many users as needed to foster teamwork. [cite: 3, 1049, 1051]</li>
        <li><strong>Configurable User Roles:</strong> Permission "cherry-picking" allows DMs to structure complex approval workflows that meet specific organizational requirements. [cite: 4, 5]</li>
